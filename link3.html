In just New Zealand alone, 1 in 4 adults have experienced difficulties with mental health, and around 1 in 5 teenagers struggle with some form of mental health issue. These numbers are a powerful reminder that mental wellbeing is something many of us need support with. However, research shows that people who do at least 2.5 hours of physical activity per week are 51% more likely to report good mental wellbeing. That’s a significant boost from something as simple as moving your body.

That’s why I have designed this digital quests app to help motivate people and give them fun, easy and active ideas for a better life. The goal is to make movement feel less like a chore, or like an hour long ab workout. But to make it more of a game. Simply just 20 minutes of physical activity daily, would help achieve having 2.5 hours of physical activity for the week. When you put your mind to it, it’s simply not a lot, but it makes a big difference!

Join me and my app to make this as simplistic and as fun as possible!
