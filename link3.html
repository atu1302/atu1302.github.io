In just New Zealand, 1 in 4 adults and 1 in 5 teenagers experience mental health difficulties. But research shows people who do at least 2.5 hours of weekly physical activity are 51% more likely to report good mental wellbeing — a big boost from simply moving your body.

That's why I designed this digital quests app: to motivate people with fun, easy, active ideas. The goal is to make movement feel like a game, not a chore. Just 20 minutes of daily activity gets you to 2.5 hours per week. It's not much, but it makes a real difference.
